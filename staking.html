Ⅰ
Checkpoints

Go to staking.polygod.io

The first step is to navigate to staking.polygod.io on your browser.(make sure you bookmark it) Once on the platform, connect your wallet on the top left corner of the page. Make sure that you're connected to the Binance Smart Chain (BSC) network and have at least $5 worth of BNB in your wallet. This is necessary to complete the necessary transactions.

After connecting your wallet, you will see your GULL token balance on the platform. This balance will determine how much you can stake on the Polygod platform. It's important to note that the more tokens you stake, the higher your reward earnings will be.

Once you've confirmed that you have the necessary requirements, the next button on the staking platform will become clickable. Click on it to proceed to the next step.

Ⅱ
Amount Stake

Input the GULL amount

In this step, you can input the amount of GULL that you want to stake or instead select the max option which will automatically select the entire amount of GULL you hold in the connected wallet, after that proceed by pressing the next button.

If you face any issue, feel free to contact us here.

Ⅲ
Pre-authorization 1/2

Authorizing

In this step, you grant access to the staking smart contract to accept your $GULL, when you click approve your wallet will open for authorization, after that proceed by pressing the next button.

If you face any issue, feel free to contact us here.

Ⅳ
Pre-authorization 2/2

Confirm

In this step, you deposit the tokens into the staking contract. After this step, your tokens will be successfully staked. when you click stake your wallet will open for staking confirmation, once your transactions is confirmed, you will go to the last page for confirmation message that your tokens are staked.

If you face any issue, feel free to contact us here.
