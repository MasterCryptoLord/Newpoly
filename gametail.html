Polygod Beta V0.8

New Changes:

  • Ability to possess a soldier, and have point-of-view (POV) and ability to attack.
  • Addition of a new character.
  • Introduction of a new defense tower.

Areas for Improvement:

  • Lack of clarity on the active tab during game startup.
  • Slow walk speed while possessing characters.
  • Confusing menus, with two clicks required to enter the game.

Bugs Encountered:

  • After dying as a possessed soldier and de-possessing the body, all cards were refreshed, doubling army size.
  • Audio settings do not save when the audio is muted.
